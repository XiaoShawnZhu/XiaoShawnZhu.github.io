Introduction

 

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan, adviced by Professor Z. Morley Mao. Before joining Michigan, I received my B.S. degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2016.

My research interests lie in the areas of computer networking, mobile computing, and system security. The topics I'm currently working on include wearable and IoT systems, mobile multipath transport, millimeter wave networking, data-driven SDN, and CPS security.
