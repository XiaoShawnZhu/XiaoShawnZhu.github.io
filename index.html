Introduction

 

Xiao Zhu is currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, working with Professor Z. Morley Mao. He also works closely with Prof. Feng Qian. Before coming to Michigan, Xiao received his B.S. from Electronic Engineering of Tsinghua University in 2016.

Xiao's research interests lie in the areas of networking, mobile computing, and system security. The topics he is currently working on include:

  • Mobile multipath transport
  • Millimeter wave networking
  • Data-driven SDN
  • ICS security
  • Massive MIMO for 5G
