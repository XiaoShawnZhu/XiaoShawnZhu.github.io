Introduction

 

I'm currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, working with Professor Z. Morley Mao. Before coming to Michigan, I received my B.S. from Electronic Engineering of Tsinghua University in 2016.

My research interests include mobile computing systems, software-defined networking, and computer system security. The topics I am currently working on include:

  • Wearable and IoT devices
  • Mobile multipath transport
  • mmWave networks
  • mmWave networking
