Xiao (Shawn) Zhu xiaoshawnzhu@gmail.com

I earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Michigan. Advised by Professor Z. Morley Mao, my Ph.D. research was on developing systems and networking support for characterizing and improving emerging Internet applications.

Before my Ph.D. study, I was an undergrad at Tsinghua University, during which I spent one semester as an exchange student at National University of Singapore.

After Ph.D. graduation, I joined Google to tackle real-world challenges in delivering high-quality and low-latency video content to billions of users across the globe in a reliable and cost-effective manner.
